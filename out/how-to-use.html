How to Use Purrify

Four simple steps to stain-free confidence

Step 1: Shake Gently

Give your Purrify pen 3-4 gentle shakes before use

This activates the cleaning formula and ensures optimal performance

💡 Pro Tip: The cat cap should rattle slightly—that's normal!

Step 2: Apply Directly

Apply directly to the stain with gentle pressure

Cover the entire stained area with a thin, even layer

💡 Pro Tip: Don't oversaturate—a little goes a long way!

Step 3: Wait 2-3 Minutes

Let the formula work its magic

The active ingredients need time to break down the stain molecules

💡 Pro Tip: Perfect time to grab a tissue or cloth for the next step

Step 4: Dab Gently

Dab with a clean cloth or tissue

Use a blotting motion rather than rubbing to avoid spreading

💡 Pro Tip: For stubborn stains, repeat the process once more

Pro Tips for Best Results

🚀 For Fresh Stains

Act quickly! Fresh stains come out much easier than set-in ones. Keep your Purrify pen handy at all times.

🧪 For Tough Stains

For stubborn stains, apply Purrify, wait the full 3 minutes, then gently work in with your finger before dabbing.

👕 For Different Fabrics

Always test on a hidden area first. Purrify works on most fabrics, but it's better to be safe!

♻️ Storage & Care

Store at room temperature. Replace the cap tightly after use to prevent evaporation. Each pen lasts 6+ months.

Refill, Reuse, Reduce Waste

Sustainability Tips

Refill Instead of Replace

Each Purrify pen is designed to be refillable. When your pen runs low, simply purchase a refill instead of a new container. One refill = less plastic waste!

Use Every Last Drop

Store your Purrify pen upright when not in use to ensure you can use every bit of the formula. Each pen contains enough solution for 50-100 stain treatments.

Keep Your Favorite Cap

Our handcrafted cat-shaped caps are collectible and reusable. When you refill, you keep your favorite cap design. Collect different designs to match your style!

Did you know? By choosing refillable Purrify, you're helping reduce plastic waste. If every student used refillable products, we could save thousands of containers from landfills each year!
